High School Diploma

The high school diploma is typically studied for over the course of four years, from grade 9 to grade 12.

Certificate Program

Certificates are available in skilled trades, career-oriented fields, and academic fields. Certificate programs usually take between one and two years to complete.

Associate's Degree

An associate degree translates into the first two years of a bachelor’s degree (freshman and sophomore years). Associate's degrees usually take between two and three years to complete.

Bachelor's Degree

A bachelor’s is a post-secondary undergraduate degree. Bachelor's degrees usually take between four and six years to complete.

Master's Degree

A master’s degree is the first level of graduate study. Master's degrees usually take between six and eight years to complete.

Doctorate Degree

A doctorate degree is the highest level of academic degree. Doctorate degrees usually take at least eight years to complete.
